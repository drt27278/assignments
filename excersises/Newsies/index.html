The Joker Has Last Laugh!

GOTHAM CITY, The Joker is behind bars this morning! The JOker was apprehended early this morning by none other than "The Batman". After about three and a half hours of high speed chases and gunfire The Joker was taken into custody by Batman, beaten and with injuries over the majority of his body he was handed over to Commissioner Gordon who then personally trasnported The Joker to the secure medical wing at the infamous Arkham Asylum. The Joker has had many many previous stays at the state hospital and somehow managed to escape every incarceration in the facility. Some have even begun to question whether The Batman has been in cahoots witnh the criminal mastermind. As always batman was not available for questioning, Comm. Gordon's response when asked the question was: "I like my rib cage in one piece, ask him yourself." We were unable to get an interview with any members of the GCPD or with the staff at Arkham. The citizens of Gotham are in an uproar, we demand to know if this will finally be the last straw for the Joker, will he be put away for good this time or is this just the same record we've been dealing with for the past two decades! When will they decide that he needs to be sentenced to the death penalty and put in the rearview mirror for good!
